Services

If you've got a large collection of unsorted vinyl, our community can help to coordinate it's donation. These donations are tax deductible and will be featured in our community section.
Our service is built off of a proprietary deep learning technology which parses images of record collections then cross references the covers with information about the records rarity and heritage status. This way we can prevent the loss of history, and help users maintain their precious collections!
