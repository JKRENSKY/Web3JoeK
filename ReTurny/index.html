Our Mission

Got a box of records collecting dust in the garage?


Why not get those records turning again!


At ReTurny our mission is to connect those with unused collections of records and equiptment with communities who lack access to vinyl music!
Through the use of deep learning and image recognition we are able to scan images of a users collection directly into our platform and create inventories with information like rarity and run.


The Vinyl Legacy

Preservation

Every year thousands of good records go unlistened to, we seek to find these wayward albums a home and bring music to the world! A frighteningly small fraction of records has been digitized, which means when a record is thrown away it could also be history going into the can.
