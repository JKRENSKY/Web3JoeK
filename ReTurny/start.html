Let's Get Started!

ReTurny is the exciting new way to get those old records out of your dusty garage, and into the hands of communities and individuals who would treasure them.
By donating albums through ReTurny you can help keep vinyl history and culture alive while also cleaning up your cluttered collection of unused records.
ReTurny strives to ensure that all records get the attention they deserve, and don't go to waste. If you aren't quite ready to start why not join our mailing list to keep up with our goings on?

Step 1 - Decide On Donation

Whether youve inherited a collection that's just not for you, or you're passing along all those $1 records you thought you'd listen to at least once, or even just getting a box out of the garage, as long as your records are in decent condition we will take them!
Gather up all the albums you'd like to donate and take a picture of them, you may take as many as you like, but please make sure you have the album art visible for each.

Step 2 - Lights, Camera, Inventory!

Gather up all of the records you are planning to donate in a well lit room, please inspect for any broken or unusable albums! We recommend placing them on a blanket or soft rug.
This is also a great excuse to play some music while you work!
Once you have them tidy and are sure these are the ones you will be posting, you can start organizing the albums to take pictures of. You can place as many as you like in a single picture as long as the covers are clearly visible
Our deep learning algorithim will parse your images to quickly identify the albums in your collection, but this depends on the cover being clearly shown in your pictures.

Step 3 - Sit Back And Let AI Handle It

Gather up all of the records you are planning to donate in a well lit room, please inspect for any broken or unusable albums! We recommend placing them on a blanket or soft rug.
This is also a great excuse to play some music while you work!

Step 4 - Check Out What You've Got!

Once our algorithim finishes with your batch, you will receive an email with a spreadsheet of all the contents.

Step 5 - Decide What To Do With It

Now that you know what's in your collection, you can decide whether to donate, sell, or keep!
Collections within zones listed on our services page are handled by volunteers within the communities of those regions, other regions to come soon!
